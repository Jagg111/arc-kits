About This Weapon
Description

A high-agility SMG that shines in PVP as one of the highest sustained damage outputs among common weapons. Its spray and horizontal recoil make long-range fights messy, but in close quarters it melts shields and players alike. With upgraded and modded it becomes surprisingly competitive with higher-tier gear in tight engagements.

⚠️ Weakness

12 rounds vanish instantly

Attachment Intel
Tips

Muzzle Brakes can feel great for no-recoil in the first 1-2 mags, but mid/long range shots become ineffective due to bloom. Learn to shoot with Compensators and avoid Muzzle Brakes.

Avoid
  • Muzzle Brakes — bloom kills mid-range accuracy
  • Extended Barrel — pointless on SMG
  • Vertical Grips — horizontal recoil is the issue, not vertical
