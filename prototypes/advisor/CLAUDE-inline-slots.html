Design notes: Crafting filters use green-tinted pills (distinct from the orange accent pills and the multi-color rarity pills) to visually separate the "what materials do I have" axis from the "what weapons do I want" axis. Each weapon block shows the full slot layout inline — no clicks required. "Not needed" slots are dimmed but still visible so you see all slots at a glance. Build name appears as a small label to identify which guide build was auto-selected.
