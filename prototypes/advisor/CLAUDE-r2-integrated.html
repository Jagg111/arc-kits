R2-C notes: Mods are shown as compact rarity-tinted chips that wrap naturally. The weapon image, name, rarity badge, build label, and mod chips are all in one cohesive block — no separate "slot list" section. This keeps cards shorter. On mobile, mod names abbreviate naturally since the chips wrap. Empty slots show as dim "Slot: —" chips. This approach trades some readability for density — good if cards staying compact matters more than full mod name visibility.
