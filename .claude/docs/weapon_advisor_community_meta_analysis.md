## Community Tier Lists (Consolidated)

### PvP Tier List

| Tier | Weapons | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| **S** | Venator, Renegade, Anvil, Stitcher, Tempest, Il Toro, Vulcano, Bobcat | Core PvP meta. Venator fires two bullets for cost of one. Renegade is peek-fighting king. Anvil is best all-rounder. Stitcher has fastest budget TTK. Bobcat is S-tier only when fully upgraded. |
| **A** | Kettle, Ferro, Torrente, Burletta | Kettle was nerfed (600 to 450 RPM in patch 1.11). Ferro punches above its weight for a Common. Torrente is a sleeper pick. Burletta is a hidden gem with huge mag potential. |
| **B** | Osprey, Bettina, Rattler | Osprey is polarizing — some rank S-tier for unique scope, others call it F-tier due to tunnel vision. Bettina struggles in PvP. Rattler hindered by poor magazine. |
| **C** | Arpeggio, Aphelion, Jupiter, Equalizer | Arpeggio lacks teeth. Legendaries have PvP vulnerabilities. |
| **D/F** | Hairpin, Hullcracker | Hairpin does 20 dmg/shot — "shoots NERF darts." Hullcracker is ARC-only. |

### PvE / ARC Tier List

| Tier | Weapons | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| **S** | Hullcracker, Jupiter, Equalizer, Anvil, Ferro, Renegade, Bettina | Hullcracker is undisputed ARC king. Jupiter has highest single-shot impact. Anvil has "Strong" armor pen at Uncommon rarity. Ferro is the budget ARC killer. |

## Community-Recommended Weapon Pairings

### Budget / Starter
- **Ferro + Stitcher** — Most recommended budget combo. Open with Ferro's massive single shot, swap to Stitcher for full-auto cleanup. Both Common rarity.
- **Il Toro + Ferro** — Il Toro dominates close range, Ferro picks at distance.
- **Kettle + Stitcher** — Both Light ammo, simplifies inventory.

### Mid-Tier / Versatile
- **Anvil + Stitcher** — Anvil covers mid-to-long, Stitcher handles CQC. Strong all-distance pairing.
- **Anvil + Tempest** — Pop shields with Anvil, finish with Tempest's fire rate.
- **Venator + Renegade** — Both Medium ammo, close-to-long coverage.
- **Renegade + Stitcher** — Renegade opens at range, Stitcher finishes in CQC.

### High-Investment / Endgame
- **Osprey + Anvil** — Snipe at range, Anvil covers CQC and anti-armor.
- **Osprey + Vulcano/Bobcat** — Control past 50m, delete pushers up close.

### ARC Farming
- **Anvil/Ferro/Jupiter (armor breaker) + Tempest/Bobcat (clear gun)**

## Map-Specific Community Recommendations

### Spaceport (Long Sightlines)
- **Primary**: Osprey or Renegade (long-range dominance essential)
- **Secondary**: Il Toro, Stitcher, or Vulcano (for building interiors)
- Community quote: "Spaceport is really empty, where long-range fights are really long, and close-range combat is really close."

### Stella Montis (Dense Close-Quarters)
- **Full CQC loadout**: Il Toro, Stitcher, Bobcat, Vulcano, or Tempest
- Community quote: "Stella Montis is all about chaos and close-range combat."
- **Avoid**: Long-range weapons like Osprey or Renegade are wasted here.

### Blue Gate (Open, High-Threat PvE)
- **Versatile picks**: Anvil, Venator, or Tempest
- **Legendary**: Equalizer specifically recommended here — "take the Equalizer when fighting on Blue Gate"
- Mix of open terrain and underground close-quarters combat.

### Dam (Mixed Lanes, Mid-Range)
- **Primary**: Anvil ("the undisputed king of Dam Battlegrounds") + Stitcher
- **Alternative**: Osprey or Renegade for rooftop fights
- Wide-open areas across hills/forests with interior building spaces.

### Buried City (Tight Spaces)
- **Primary**: Osprey (for rooftop fights) + Anvil (for tight corners)
- **CQC alternatives**: Il Toro, Stitcher, Vulcano
- Packed building layout with prevalent camping.

## Solo vs Squad Loadout Differences

### Solo Priorities
- **Mobility over firepower** — Heavy rifles/LMGs "sap mobility, which can be fatal for solo players who must escape on their own."
- Mid-to-long range weapon + close-range finisher for self-sufficiency at all ranges.
- Medium Shield (Blue) for best balance of durability and movement speed.

### Squad Priorities
- **Role specialization** — long-range marksman, mid-range anchor, support player.
- Can afford heavier weapons — Torrente LMG becomes viable with teammates covering flanks.
- Coordinate ammo types — avoid everyone running same ammo.

---

## Stealth Meta

- **Hairpin** is the only silenced weapon (built-in). F-tier combat, but legitimate stealth utility for cameras/alarms/ticks.
- Community consensus: stealth is not heavily prioritized in the meta. The game rewards efficiency over stealth.
- Renegade "works beautifully with a silencer" for stealth play.
- Pair stealth weapon with a real combat weapon for when stealth fails.

## Flagged Weak Picks in Current Golden Case Pools

### S04 — `expectedPrimaryPool: ["tempest", "renegade", "rattler", "torrente"]`
- **Rattler** — C-tier PvP, C-tier ARC. Called "jack-of-all-situations, master of none, outclassed by most." 12-round mag with 2-bullet reload widely criticized. **Consider removing from primary pool.**
- **Torrente** — B-tier PvP but "solo players typically skip it." Borderline for squads but niche.

### S05 — `expectedSecondaryPool: ["rattler", "burletta", "kettle", "iltoro"]`
- **Burletta** — D-tier PvP, D-tier ARC. Only valued for fastest draw speed. **Consider replacing with Ferro.**

### S06 — `expectedPrimaryPool: ["osprey", "renegade", "tempest", "arpeggio"]`
- **Arpeggio** — D-tier PvP, C-tier ARC. "Most people scrap it for parts." Dramatically outclassed by Renegade/Tempest/Osprey which all also have Muzzle slots. **Strong candidate for removal.**

### S02 — `expectedSecondaryPool: ["renegade", "tempest", "venator", "ferro", "vulcano", "bobcat"]`
- **Ferro** (PvP:C) — Break-action doesn't suit CQC PvP at Stella Montis. **Consider removing.**
- **Renegade** — Community explicitly says long-range weapons are "wasted" at Stella Montis. **Consider removing.**

### S03 — `expectedSecondaryPool: ["anvil", "ferro", "tempest", "renegade"]`
- **Tempest** (ARC:C) — Weak for a PvE-focused scenario. **Consider removing or replacing with Hullcracker.**
- **Renegade** (ARC:B) — Borderline. Decent but not optimal for PvE.

## Key Takeaways

1. **Anvil is king** — primary or secondary in almost every scenario. Community consensus: best all-around weapon.
2. **Renegade + Anvil is THE meta pair** for long-range PvP — different ammo types, complementary ranges, both A-tier+.
3. **Bobcat and Vulcano dominate CQC PvP** but lack muzzle slots — tension with stealth scenarios.
4. **Jupiter + Equalizer** is the clear Legendary PvE pairing.
5. **Stitcher + Anvil/Ferro** is the consensus budget combo.
6. **Stealth barely changes optimal picks** — reinforces Renegade (silencer-compatible) over alternatives.
7. **Weak picks to prune**: Rattler (S04), Arpeggio (S06), Burletta (S05), Ferro & Renegade (S02), Tempest (S03).
